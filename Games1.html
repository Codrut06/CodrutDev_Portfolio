Pong AI Challenge: An Intelligent Take on a Classic Game

Pong AI Challenge is a Python game using the Pygame library, where players face an AI opponent. The AI dynamically analyzes the ball’s trajectory and predicts its future position, adjusting the paddle to block player shots. Unique to this game is the adaptive difficulty: as players score, the AI becomes faster and more precise, enhancing the challenge. Real-time calculations make matches unpredictable and engaging. With retro-style visuals and smooth gameplay, Pong AI Challenge modernizes the classic game by focusing on intelligent opponent behavior, featuring customizable difficulty settings to suit different skill levels.
