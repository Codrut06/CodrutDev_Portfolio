Passionate About Learning and Growth

As someone who is just beginning my journey, I bring a fresh perspective and an eagerness to learn. I’m excited to contribute to innovative projects and collaborate with others to find creative solutions.

Embracing Challenges and Learning Opportunities

In a constantly evolving technological landscape, I am dedicated to helping others navigate the challenges of learning programming and emerging technologies. I offer programming consulting for beginners, covering languages such as Python, C++, Java, and HTML/CSS, enabling those at the start of their journey to acquire the necessary knowledge to begin coding. Additionally, I share my experiences to provide mentoring in robotics, focusing on the First Tech Challenge (FTC). I assist students in building and programming robots, as well as preparing for the competition, offering insights into teamwork, technical challenges, and competition strategy.

  • Programming Consulting for Beginners – Offering guidance in programming languages such as Python, C++, Java, and HTML/CSS to help beginners grasp the fundamentals and get started on their coding journey.
  • Mentoring in Robotics and First Tech Challenge – Providing mentorship in building and programming robots for the FTC competition, guiding teams through coding, mechanical design, and competition strategy.
  • Organizing Coding Workshops – Creating and leading coding workshops for peers or younger students, using interactive sessions to build and enhance programming skills.

I also collaborate with students and peers on group projects, facilitating a cooperative learning environment where we can tackle technical challenges together. Beyond this, I manage small tech projects, applying agile project management principles such as Scrum and Kanban, ensuring effective organization and execution.
